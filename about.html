Who We Are

Meet the Team

CTIR is made up of Cal Poly students from across engineering disciplines who share a passion for the coast. Together they design, deploy, and maintain tide gauges along the Central Coast of California.

Charlie Newcomb

Water Resources Engineering, M.S.

I've been helping out with the tide gauge project since spring quarter 2025. I've enjoyed getting outside and retrieving data from the sensors in Morro Bay and the Cal Poly Pier in Avila. This year I'm helping out by coordinating meetings and guiding overall efforts for the quality assurance team. I'm looking forward to diving deeper into this research project as I finish my master's degree in the upcoming spring!

Sebastian Bach

Civil Engineering, 3rd Year

Most of my work is out in the field setting up some of the sensors around the San Luis Obispo area. My favorite memory so far has been setting up the sensor in the Los Osos marsh with Serena. Since the tide was a low king tide we had to wade through mud to get to the right place — sometimes almost knee deep in it.
